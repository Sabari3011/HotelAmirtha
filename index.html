Our sucess story

The success story of Amirtha Restaurant began with a journey rooted in dedication and passion. With 24 years of experience honed through a craft course and the establishment of Sri Vaigai Decors, coupled with 15 years of expertise in catering services across four marriage halls with capacity of serving 50 -5000 peoples at a time and successfully serving more than 6000 events followed by 300+ events with overall event management service especially some of the events were planed and done with in 24 hours, we build  our team to serving people with quality service at affordable prices. However, realizing that serving only at special  occasions not quench our thirst, we embarked on a new venture . Thus, Amirtha Restaurant was born with the help of respectful Chairman Maheshkumar . To  provide wholesome meals for atleast 500 people daily, ensuring satisfaction for every palate without compromising on quality at affordable budget and  became a new identity of kangeyam by organaizing Restaurant, Bakery, Lodge, Party Hall all  at one place in the center of kangeyam.
